Privacy Policy for EastStay Vendor
Information We Collect

When you create an account as a hotel owner, we collect information such as your name, email address, and contact details.

To add your hotel to our platform, we collect information about your hotel, including its name, location, amenities, room types, and other relevant details.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used to create and display your hotel listing on EastStay.

We may use your contact information to communicate with you about your hotel listing, updates, and relevant services.

We use aggregated and anonymized data to analyze trends and improve the functionality of our hotel owner platform.

Information Sharing

Your hotel information, excluding sensitive details, may be displayed publicly on EastStay.

We may share your information with third-party service providers to facilitate the operation of our platform.

Gallery Access and Data Storage

Our app, EastStay Vendor, may request access to your device's gallery to allow you to upload room pictures for your hotel listings. We respect your privacy, and this access is solely for the purpose of enhancing your experience within the app.

The images you select from your gallery are stored securely in Firebase Storage, ensuring the integrity and availability of your room pictures. Additionally, we may store some data locally on your device to improve the performance and personalized features of the app, such as user preferences or cached content.

We want to assure you that we do not access, read, or transfer any personal or sensitive information from your device without your explicit consent.

Room Location Information

EastStay Vendor allows users to specify the location of their hotel rooms by either manually inputting the location or selecting it on a map integrated with Mapbox. The location information provided by users is used to enhance the accuracy and relevance of hotel listings on EastStay.

We want to assure you that we respect your privacy, and the collection of room location information is solely for the purpose of improving the accuracy and quality of your hotel listings within the app. This location data is not shared with third parties without your explicit consent, and we do not use it for any other purposes beyond enhancing the functionality of EastStay Vendor.

Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal and hotel information.

You can opt-out of promotional communications.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please review it periodically for any changes.
